About Me

Jazmaigne Gaza

Hi I'm Jaz! I am not a professional photographer, I am just a dreamer, who spends a lot taking pictures of myself, nature and almost everything. I just love taking pictures as my recollection of memories for my travels. I have loved photography since I was a teenager growing up travelling with my family. I naturally learned how much photography plays an important role in my life- It connects to every memories, it reminds me of people, places, feelings, and stories. I find beauty in the things and people around, helps me to see things in a different light. A wonderful therapeutic benefit of photography, is that it can help me see the beauty surrounding us we just have take a moment to stop and look!

Please document your life. Take pictures and videos or keep a journal. The most insignificant moments will become some of your favourite memories one day. Capture each core memory. Embrace your experiences and share your perspective. You'll love looking back at the memories, reliving the journey, and cherishing them for a lifetime.
